Blockchain and Smart Contracts

Blockchain

A distributed ledger technology that allows transactions to be recorded in many different places at the same time.

Nick Sabo

Smart Contract

Programmed contract whose agreements are written in computer code on the blockchain. The contract is automatically executed without an intermediary (trust in the intermediary). These agreements can always be viewed, but cannot be changed.

Smart contracts are contracts that are

  • are completely digital;
  • consist of computer code;
  • are programmed on a blockchain;
  • are automatically executed without an intermediary;
  • are impossible to modify.
